Whats Left Behind.

Date: 2022

Dimensions: 60" x 38"

What's Left Behind is a series of wire sculpture pieces that represent the consuming feeling that materialistic items can bring.

I began this project with the intention of combining my love for fashion and how it plays a role in my life. This series is created using steel, aluminum, and black tuille. Using a wire wrapping, all joints are creating with no braising or welding. The process of making these pieces required a difficult and painful manipulation of the materials, creating a stark contrast with the stereotypical feminine pieces that were chosen.
