Human Disruption

Date: 2023

Dimensions: 36" x 18" x 12"

Human Disruption is an acrylic piece, with a movement recognition system controlled by an Arduino Uno.

This acrylic sculpture is an attempt to display my experience of my mental chaos and its relationship with emotional closeness. The top pieces represent trees in their natural state, with the underneath wiring representing its roots. As I approach closer to my natural state of chaos, the world around me spins in an uncontrollable way. Being a multi-disciniplinary artist and conjoining my academic disciplines allowed for the creation of new opportunities like this project.

How It Works: A motion sensor is placed inside the box to detect an audience. Depending on the distance of the person, the forest landscape will change it's speed. As the person approaches closer, the motors spin the scenery at a faster speed , demonstrating different levels of chaos.
