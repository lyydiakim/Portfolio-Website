Generational Dichotomies

Date: 2023

Dimensions: 36" x 36"

Generation Dichotomies is a lighting and woodworking piece that is inspired by my experience growing up in a multi-generational, Korean home.

The symmetrical line patterns present in my piece are inspired by traditional Korean homes, called Hanok ~ 한옥, that were popularly built during the 14th century Joseon dynasty. I utilize the long heritage of this line pattern, in contrast with a wooden crack, to symbolize a break away from what it meant for me to be a "good asian".
